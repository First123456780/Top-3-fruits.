Welcome to the Top 3 Fruits Homepage

1. Banana

Bananas are sweet, soft fruits that are rich in potassium, which helps keep your heart and muscles healthy. They're easy to digest and give you quick energy.

2. Apple

Apples are crisp, juicy fruits that are high in fiber and vitamin C. They help with digestion and support a healthy immune system.

3. Orange

Orange is a sweet, juicy fruit that comes from the citrus tree. It's round, usually orange in color, and rich in vitamin C. Oranges can be eaten fresh or used to make juice. They are popular worldwide for their refreshing taste and health benefits.
